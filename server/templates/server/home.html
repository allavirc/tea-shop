About the producer

Refreshing, invigorating, robust or soothing, our love of tea permeates everything we do: from blending in our own factory in Sri Lanka, all the way to your freshly brewed cup. But tea is much more than that. We care about everyone in our supply chain. From pickers, packers, mixers to drinkers. Small plantations, organic, fair trade and sustainable are words that fit the organization. Not only for the impact they have on the company, but also for generations to come.
